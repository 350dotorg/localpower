The Vampire Hunt

On Halloween night, you’ll probably see lots of vampire costumes. But there are real vampires in your home right now – energy vampires. Every day, “vampire electronics” suck energy and money away from your home. Only you can put a stop to this terrifying energy waste. Take action with Repower at Home to slay these vampires, and recruit your friends to join your hunting party. Can you recruit the most vampire slayers by Halloween?

What is vampire power?

Your electronics use energy even when they’re switched “off” to power things like sensors and lights. In fact, the average electronic consumes most of its energy when you’re not even using it. Every month, you spend about 10% of your electricity bill feeding these vampires. Learn more about vampire power.

Free your community

Your home may be vampire-free, but what about your friends? Only you can save them from the vampires!

Take it to the streets

Recruit your friends to slay their vampires and watch your standing rise on our leader board. The more people you inspire to take action, the higher your standing in the slayer community. Recruitment is easy:

  1. Download and print out materials for recruiting slayers. These materials will turn any Joe Schmoe into a vampire-slaying machine.
    • Commitment card
    • Slayer Guide
    • Halloween decorations
  2. Talk to friends, neighbors and community members about slaying vam- pires. Knock on doors in your neighborhood. Set up a table at your kid’s school. Anytime you meet people you can help them save energy and money.
  3. Have potential slayers fill out the commitment card and hand it back to you.
  4. Give your newly-recruited slayer the Slayer Guide and the Halloween Decoration.
  5. Enter the commitment card info on the website and watch your ranking rise on the leader board.
