We did it!

This summer, we set an ambitious goal: inspire Americans to save the equivalent of seven million pounds of coal by 10/10. Thanks to Trendsetters like you, not only did we meet that goal, but we surpassed it. On 10/10, a global day of action, we joined thousands of people around the world in sending a clear message: we’re not waiting for solutions to climate change – we’re getting to work.

To commemorate our achievement, we’re giving all of our Trendsetters a free sticker, identifying you as someone who is committed to blazing the trail forward.

Together, we are laying the groundwork for an enormous transformation in how we consume and think about energy. But we’re just at the beginning of this movement. The Trendsetter sticker isn’t just a reward for being on the cutting edge of climate action; it’s also a powerful way to encourage others to join us.

When your friends and neighbors see that you’re taking action to save energy, it inspires them to get involved. Maybe your neighbor wants to save energy, but keeps putting it off. Seeing that you’re already taking action will help her move from someone who is on the sidelines, to someone who is taking action and making a difference.

That’s why we’re asking you to put your sticker someplace visible. Great places to show off your Trendsetter status include you laptop, your favorite water bottle, your mailbox, your recycling bin and your car. Once you put up the sticker, take a picture of it and email it back to us. We love seeing the creative places that Trendsetters are using to get the message out.

Thank you again for helping us reach our goal. We’re often a great start, and with your help, we can make a huge difference.
