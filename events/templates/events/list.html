Host an energy meeting

Energy meetings let you introduce your friends, coworkers, and neighbors to the benefits of saving energy. These fun events teach participants about key energy actions and get them excited about saving energy as a team.

Repower at Home will provide all the materials you need. You don’t have to be an expert in energy efficiency to host an event.

Who would I invite?

You’re already plugged into many networks: your office, your children’s school, your bowling team, and so on. Who isn’t interested in cleaner air and better homes? Many hosts are unsure in the beginning, but find it easy to develop a guest list of well over thirty people! The host materials will help you think through the process and develop a guest list.
