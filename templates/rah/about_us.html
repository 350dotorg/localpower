About Us

Repower at Home is a new project of the Alliance for Climate Protection, a national nonprofit. Repower at Home is dedicated to empowering individuals and communities to save energy. By using online tools and in-person community meetings, we encourage people to take an active role in reducing energy waste. Together, we can build a cleaner tomorrow.
